Snakes and Ladders

With a vibrant 10x10 board and two vibrant players, the battleground is set for an exhilarating showdown between the fiery Red and the cool Blue.
Features
  1. Customize your gaming experience with the sound ON/OFF button.
  2. Toggle between sound effects to suit your preferences and enjoy uninterrupted gameplay.
  3. Enjoy Snake and Ladders anywhere, anytime by downloading in your computer - no internet connection required!

This desktop application is perfect for:
  1. Family game nights
  2. Quick bursts of nostalgic fun
  3. Introducing the classic game to a new generation

Technologies Used:
  1. Python 3
  2. Tkinter
  3. Pygame (Audio library)
