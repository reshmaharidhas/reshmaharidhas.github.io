PC Musique Player

Play songs in your computer with this feature rich and themed music player

Features

  1. Music Playback
  2. Next and Previous buttons to change audio to play in selected library.
  3. Displays album art, artist name, album name,album year and duration
  4. Adjustable slider to show progress of the audio and to change playing from specific second.
  5. Select the library folder by click of button
  6. Double click on audio files in library to play
  7. Control player to play, pause and resume by pressing space bar key.
  8. Control volume within player
  9. Mute playback by single click in controls.
  10. Repeat audio to play in loop
  11. Play songs in library by sorting based on album name, audio file name, album year.
  12. Play songs in library by shuffling.
  13. Player plays all songs in the library continuously.
  14. Change player to compact size by hiding library.
  15. 16 different themes for a variety of visual experience of player


Download software:
Windows
Technologies Used:
  1. Python 3
  2. Tkinter
  3. Pygame (Audio library)
